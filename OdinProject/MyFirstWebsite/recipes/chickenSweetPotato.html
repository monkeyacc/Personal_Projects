    • Chicken & Sweet Potato

    Description:

    What we have here is a simple but powerful meal. It's very well balanced, flavor-wise. Please ignore the egg & spinach, it was leftovers.

    Ingredients:

    • 1 medium sized skinless/boneless chicken breast
    • 1 medium sized sweet potato
    • Chicken seasoning
    • Salt and pepper
    • Homemade salsa

    Steps:

    1. Slap that chicken down onto a cutting board, slice it hamburger way and season it with some "chicken" seasoning
    2. Next, we're going to drizzle a bit of extra virgin olive oil onto a hot pan and cook the chicken breast.
    3. Next, we will wash and cut the sweet potato into slices, placing them onto a microwave-safe plate and heat them up for 4 minutes.
    4. Now that our chicken breast is cooked, we can slice them into slices.
    5. Once the sweet potato is soft and lightly seasoned, we can enjoy with our chicken breast and some homemade salsa.
    6. For the most important step, enjoy!
