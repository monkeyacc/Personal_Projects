    • Yogurt and Fruit

    Description:

    Pictured above is my favorite snack, hands down! It's delicious, easily digestible and is packing some serious gains (protein).

    Ingredients:

    • 1 - 1.5 cups of plain nonfat Greek yogurt
    • 1 medium sized banana (sliced)
    • 8 medium sized strawberries

    Steps:

    1. Let's first take our serving(s) of plain nonfat Greek yogurt in a small bowl
    2. Next, let's wash our strawberries and blueberries (if applicable) and slice our strawberries in half while also removing the stem
    3. Then, we will slice our banana and add it do our bowl of yogurt
    4. Finally, we will add the rest of our berries into our bowl and enjoy!
