    • Chocolate Protein Shake

    Description:

    So this is one of my favorite snacks: a chocolate protein shake.

    Ingredients:

    • 1 scoop (25g) of your favorite chocolate powder protein
    • 6 - 8 ice cubes depending on their size (if big 6, if medium sized 8)
    • 1 medium sized banana
    • 6 fluid ounces of water
    • I occasionally add some fruit to switch it up, usually strawberries or blueberries

    Steps:

    1. Let's take tour blender and first add your scoop of protein powder followed by the ice cubes
    2. Breaking up the banana by hand into pieces, let's also add that to our blender
    3. With this third step, we add out water and any other berries that'd you like (try not to overdue it)
    4. Blend the ingredients until it has reached a smooth texture and enjoy!
