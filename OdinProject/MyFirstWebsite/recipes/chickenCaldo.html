    • Chicken Caldo

    Description:

    Alright alright, now this one, my girlfriend's mom showed me. (P.S. your nose may get runny while ingesting.

    Ingredients:

    • Get about 5 chicken legs and two skinless/boneless chicken breasts
    • Get some carrots
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Add some corn if you'd like
    • Get some celery in there as well
    • Some GOYA seasoning
    • Chopped onion
    • Chopped jalepeno (optional)
    • Crushed pepper for a kick
    • Lime
    • Water

    Steps:

    1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add some other seasonings also ya bloke.
    2. Similar to our beef caldo recipe, we want to first let the chicken cook first.
    3. Now, we want to wash and prep all of veggies. Let's cut them into some reasonably size portions/bite size. Throw them into the pot.
    4. Cook the veggies to desired tenderness.
    5. Once your kitchen is smelling amazing and you can tell that the caldo is done, serve it up!
    6. Add some chopped onion, chopped jalepeno, and crushed pepper for some texture and spice!
    7. Squeeze a bit of lime in there if you're into that sort of thing and enjoy!
